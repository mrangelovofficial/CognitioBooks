Description

Ever wanted to know more about The Office?

The Office is a United States TV series that transcends generations and persists in popularity through the decades. The Scranton branch of Dunder Mifflin Paper returns with more stories to tell. This time not by its regional manager Micahel Scott, or top salesman Dwight Shrute, or Jim, or Pam - but by its creators, its actors and its writers.

The author Andy Greene offers readers a behind-the-scenes insight into emblematic episodes like Dinner Party, Thread Level Midnight and more. Additionally the book features interviews with various members of the production, revealing untold stories about the show.

The book talks about the auditions and how the cast was picked. Did you know that Jenna Fisher (Pam) actually worked as a receptionist before coming onto the show? Or that Angela Kinsey (Angela) worked at a call center? Andy Greene also goes over the later seasons of The Office, both before and after Steve Carell leaves the show.

So, are you ready to return to Scranton's most popular office building (which is actually located in Los Angeles)?
