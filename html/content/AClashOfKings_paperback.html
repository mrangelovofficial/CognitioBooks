Description

A Clash Of Kings is the second book in the series 'A Song Of Ice And Fire', a franchise brought to life in HBO's Game Of Thrones TV series, which has seen massive commercial success.

As King Robert Baratheon dies, his son Joffrey ascends the Iron Throne and the Seven Kingdoms are plunged into chaos. Five pretenders to the throne emerge, summoning their loyal bannermen and preparing to march south to King's Landing as war overtakes the realm - the war of five kings. In this high stakes game of thrones, you either win or you die.

While contenders for the crown clash in Westeros, grave danger lurks north of the Great Wall as the fallen rise from the dead and march once more. An ill omen dashes across the sky, warning of a war greater than that of kings.

About George R.R. Martin

George Raymond Richard Martin was born in 1948. His professional career began in 1970 when he was 21 years old. He wrote novels, as well as screen plays and television series, while also teaching. He began writing the series 'A Song Of Ice and Fire' in 1991, which is yet to be finished as one book remains to conclude the seven volumes.
